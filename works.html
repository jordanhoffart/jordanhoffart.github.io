  • Tin ejecta simulation
    Description: A tin metal plate (light orange) with a surface defect is impulsively shocked by a copper flyer plate (red) moving upwards at a velocity of 1600m/s. The tin coupon is liquefied and mass is ejected faster near the defect than in the surrounding metal. The left side shows the density profile of the simulation while the right shows the pressure profile in a logarithmic scale.
